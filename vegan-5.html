VEGAN CAKE

A vegan cake is a type of cake that is made without any animal products or by-products, such as eggs, milk, butter, or cream. Instead, plant-based alternatives are used to create a delicious and moist cake that is suitable for those following a vegan diet. Vegan cakes can be made in a variety of flavors and styles, just like traditional cakes. Some popular ingredients for vegan cakes include non-dairy milk, such as almond milk or coconut milk, vegetable oil, apple sauce, or mashed bananas instead of eggs, and vegan butter or margarine instead of dairy butter. Vegan cakes can be decorated with vegan frosting made with plant-based ingredients such as coconut cream or vegan butter, and can be topped with fresh fruits, nuts, or vegan chocolate chips.

Ingredients



  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar

Instructions



  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease a 9-inch cake pan with non-stick spray and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt until well combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the almond milk, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, and apple cider vinegar until well combined.
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until the batter is smooth and free of lumps.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.
  6. Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool for 10-15 minutes before removing it from the pan and transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. Once the cake has cooled, you can frost it with your favorite vegan frosting, decorate it with fresh fruit or vegan whipped cream, and serve!
