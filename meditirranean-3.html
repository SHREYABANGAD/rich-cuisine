ARMENIA LOSH KEBAB

The losh kebab is a large burger made with fifty percent lamb, fifty percent beef. It is mixed with amazing herbs, spices, and chopped white onion. It’s an absolute flavor bomb. There is lots of fresh parsley and onion chopped up and mixed into the beef and lamb burgers. I also notice a slight tomatoey flavor which is from the tomato paste. For spices, the main note I taste is cumin, which is so delicious in grilled meat recipes.

Ingredients



  • 1 pound ground lamb
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/2 white onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 white onion, chopped

Instructions



  1. In a bowl, combine the ground beef, lamb, egg, 1/2 onion, 3/4 cup parsley, egg, cumin, tomato sauce/paste, lemon, olive oil, and salt and pepper. Form into burger patties and set aside.
  2. Grill the losh kebab burgers, flipping only once. You can grill these medium rare, or well, depending on your preference. Set aside to rest.
  3. Chop the other onion and parsley. Combine well in a bowl.
  4. Place a burger in a pita wrap, top with onion/parsley mixture, wrap it up, and enjoy!
