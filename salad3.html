VEGAN COBB SALAD

A vegan cobb salad is a twist on the classic cobb salad, which typically contains meat, eggs, and dairy products. This plant-based version is made entirely from vegan ingredients and is packed with nutrients and flavor. The salad typically includes a base of mixed greens, such as romaine lettuce or baby spinach, and is topped with a variety of colorful veggies such as diced tomatoes, avocado, cucumber, and shredded carrots.

Ingredients



  • 1 head of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa,1 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh chives
  • 1/2 cup vegan bacon bits (optional)

Instructions



  1. In a large salad bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce, cherry tomatoes, cooked chickpeas, cooked quinoa, diced avocado, red onion, and cucumber.
  2. Add the chopped parsley, basil, chives, and vegan bacon bits (if using) on top.
  3. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, maple syrup, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious vegan Cobb salad!
