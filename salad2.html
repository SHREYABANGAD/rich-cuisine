SHAVED BRUSSELS SPROUT SALADS

This salad is a perfect mix of savory and sweet, with the tangy lemon dressing balancing out the sweetness of the dried cranberries and the nuttiness of the walnuts. The shaved Brussels sprouts provide a crunchy texture that is both satisfying and refreshing. This salad is a great side dish or light meal that's perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients



  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions



  1. In a large bowl, combine the shaved Brussels sprouts, dried cranberries, chopped walnuts, and grated Parmesan cheese
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, Dijon mustard, honey, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  3. Pour the dressing over the Brussels sprout mixture and toss until well coated.
  4. Chill the salad for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Serve and enjoy your delicious shaved Brussels sprout salad!
